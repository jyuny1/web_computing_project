King's Road

brief introduction

This residence is situated on Brighton seafront, with many rooms offering wonderful sea views. In a central city location the residence is close to lively bars, restaurants and clubs. The self-contained flats house up to six students - with each flat having its own kitchen/diner, bath, shower and toilet facilities, with laundry facilities on site. Each bedroom has its own washbasin. Internet access provided.

Park Houses

brief introduction

The Park Village residences consist of adjoining three-storey houses which are laid out to form a pedestrian precinct. Each house consists of 12 single study bedrooms, two kitchens and bath, shower and toilet facilities. The bedrooms all have their own washbasins. The village consists of 468 single rooms, 24 small two-bed flats and 36 family flats. There is also a social centre which houses the manager's office, the porter's lodge and a large TV room and lounge area. Between August 2010 and January 2011, the Housing Office will also be based in this centre.

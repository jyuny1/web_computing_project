Brighthelm

brief introduction

The Brighthelm Residences consist of 60 five-bedroom houses. Some houses are allocated to postgraduates, and the rest to undergraduates. Houses have well equipped kitchens/diners (including washing machines and tumble driers). Each bedroom has its own washbasin and there are 2 bathrooms in each house.
